About PragyanPath

PragyanPath is a free, AI-powered education platform designed to make learning simple, fun, and accessible for students from Class 1 to 12. Our goal is to provide high-quality content for all boards in a simple language that students can easily understand.

Our Vision

To empower every student with smart, interactive, and personalized learning tools without any cost.

Our Mission

  • Provide clear, board-aligned educational content.
  • Use AI and smart technology to simplify learning.
  • Make learning accessible to all students, anywhere.
  • Keep content 100% safe, reliable, and free from ads.
